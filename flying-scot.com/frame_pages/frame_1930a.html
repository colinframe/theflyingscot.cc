MYSTERY 1930s FRAME

Though the owner has updated the components on this frame and the fork would not appear to be original, the lugwork, and use of Chater-Lea top-eyes on the seat-stays, are unmistakably 1930's. This may in fact predate frame No.2516 in the Glasgow Museum of Transport, and be one of the oldest surviving Scots.
