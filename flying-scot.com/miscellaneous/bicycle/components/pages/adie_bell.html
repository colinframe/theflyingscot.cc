Promotional Material - Flying Scot Bell
Photograph courtesy of John Gibb

Rattrays offered customers, their own version of the classic chrome-plated steel bell.

Wholesalers supplied these to shops on a minimum order basis, and in return the retailer was provided with them stamped with whatever pattern or design they chose.

Based on a standard bicycle bell by 'Adie', Rattrays naturally used them to advertise their pride and joy, the "Flying Scot"...
