'ROBERTSON' / 'MILANO'
JOHN ROBERTSON ( PARIS SPORT)



Paris Sport
647 Govan Road
Glasgow

John Robertson opened his Govan Road shop in 1950 and the following year began production of his lightweight cycles under the 'Milano' (as seen above) & later the 'Robertson' Marques. Heavilly influenced by all things from the continental racing scene, they featured much use of French and Italian componentry. Approx 1200 in all were built.
