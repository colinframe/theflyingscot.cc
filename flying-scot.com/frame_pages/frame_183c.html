183 C - 1953

Fellow Scot George MacLeod from Tillicoultry bought this Flying Scot in 1978. Originally set up & equipped (including Campagnolo gears) for racing, George altered it to make it more appropriate for touring, including fitting Mavic clincher rims and a wider ratio freewheel block (14-28). When he acquired it it was finished in two-tone blue, though there were no transfers in place.
