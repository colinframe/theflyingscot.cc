Mystery Frames

Such was the popularity and allure of the Flying Scot's that from time to time mystery frames pop up. These are frames that don't quite fit the bill - some that look not unlike a Scot, but are missing some of the wee Rattray's "signature" details that appear in almost all of the frames produced by them. Often they come with some kind of provenance - it came from a reputable dealer who should know - the original owner said it was a Scot - and so on.

So are they Flying Scot's ?

Have a look for yourself. These are all frames which have come with the tag "Flying Scot". If you think you know what they are and can identify them, please get in touch!
