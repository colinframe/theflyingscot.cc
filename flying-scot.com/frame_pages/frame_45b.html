45 B - 1952

This Scot had lain for a number of years in an attic, and had suffered the not only the ravages of time but also various different disguises as can be seen here. Traces of the original colour on which the restoration was based were found on the fork steerer tube. Of particular note is the use of the long "flags" badge on the head tube. Clearly the cat wasn't too impressed at this stage.





Repainted in it's original colour, the frame is seen here shortly after it's return from M.Steel Cycles Ltd.
