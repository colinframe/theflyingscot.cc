1937 Advert
In the 1930's, the McNeil Brothers John & Andy produced some fine lightweight machines at 925 Govan Road, Glasgow. Andy continued to build them for a short period after the war, and they were sold under his own 'Andy McNeil' name. Though the above machine was thought to date from 1948, the frame number '446.2' is perhaps more indicative of one built two years earlier.
